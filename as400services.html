I-series service

AS400 is a familiar name for people who were in the computer business in 1980’s.

IBM i is an integrated operating environment with a more than 20-year reputation for exceptional security and business resilience.

 IBM i (formerly known as i5/OS) running on an IBM Power Systems server offers a highly scalable and virus resistant architecture with a proven reputation for exceptional business resiliency. Running applications based on i has helped companies over many years to focus on innovation and delivering new value to their business, not just on managing their data center operations.

DB2 for i (formerly known as DB2 for i5/OS) is an advanced, 64-bit Relational Database Management System (RDBMS) that leverages the high performance, virtualization, and energy efficiency features of IBM's Power Systems.

Dilan Enterprises product groups include a full line of Mainframe and AS400 solutions covering all aspects of Data Center Management including JCL Asset Management, DASD Data Management, Output Management, Tape Media Management, Console Automation and iSeries / AS400 Data Compression, Encryption, Security, and Messaging.
