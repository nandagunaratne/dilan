On site Delivery Model
Services are delivered ‘on-site’ at the client location by an expert of our team. Entire project management, from the initial project study, implementation, to maintenance and support, is performed in continuous association with the client, at the client site.

Offshore Delivery Model
Entire project development, from initial Study phase to Testing, is carried out at Dilan Enterprise’s offshore facility. Project management and communication aspects are controlled by Program managers who are internal client representatives. An expert team is deployed at the offshore development centre to execute the project

Hourly Rate Model
Hourly rate model is ideal for trial projects or one time projects whose goals and specifications are clearly defined and the possibility of deviation from the project is unlikely.
